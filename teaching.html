Teaching Physics, Mathematics, and ICT

As a tutor, I have taught physics, mathematics, and information and communication technology (ICT) to students of classes 9-10 and HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) level. My teaching approach emphasizes:

  • Conceptual Understanding: Focusing on building a strong foundation of concepts in physics, mathematics, and ICT.
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Helping students develop analytical and problem-solving skills through practice and application of concepts.
  • Interactive Learning: Engaging students in interactive sessions to encourage active participation and deeper understanding.

My goal is to empower students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their academic pursuits and beyond.
